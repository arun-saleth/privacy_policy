Privacy Policy

Budgy: Budget Planner ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or phone number unless you provide it directly.
  • Financial Data: Your budgeting and expense data are stored locally on your device by default. However, if you enable Google Drive backup, your data will be backed up to Google Drive for the purpose of syncing and restoring your app data across devices.
  • Google Drive Data: We only store your budgeting and expense data on Google Drive and it is not shared with any third parties. No personal information (such as your name, email, or any other details) is collected through Google Drive.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information (such as device model and operating system) anonymously to improve app performance. This information is not linked to you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality.
  • To sync and back up your data with Google Drive (if enabled) to ensure your data is safe and can be restored across devices.
  • To improve the app based on anonymous usage statistics (if collected).

3. Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your budgeting and expense data are stored locally on your device.
  • Google Drive Backup: If you enable Google Drive backup, your data will be securely stored on Google Drive. You can disable this feature at any time in the app settings.
  • Google's Privacy Policy: Data stored in Google Drive is subject to Google's own Privacy Policy. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Drive: We use Google Drive as a cloud backup service. By enabling backup, your data will be stored on Google's servers. Google may collect information as outlined in their own privacy policy, but we do not have access to or collect any personal data through Google Drive.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app or on this page.
